Silica Granules: Versatile and Essential

Silica Granules, produced from high-purity quartz, are a key material for water filtration, foundry, glass, ceramics, and landscaping. Their controlled size, shape, and purity ensure optimal performance in every application.

"From clean water to strong castings, silica granules are the backbone of modern industry."

Why Choose VIKOMO Silica Granules?

  • High Purity: Sourced and processed for maximum SiO₂ content and minimal impurities.
  • Consistent Whiteness: Ensures brightness and clarity in end products.
  • Controlled Granule Size: Available in a wide range of sizes and shapes.
  • Low Iron Content: Strict quality control for Fe₂O₃ and other contaminants.
